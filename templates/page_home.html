Simple Process, Powerful Results

Get started in minutes with our intuitive workflow

For Lecturers

  1. Set Up Your Courses – create courses, add students and link device identifiers.
  2. Start a Session – open the attendance page, select your course and click “Take Attendance”.
  3. Scan & Review – the system detects student devices and marks attendance automatically.
  4. Export Reports – download attendance in PDF, Excel or CSV with a single click.

For Students

  1. Enable Bluetooth – turn on Bluetooth on your phone before entering class.
  2. Open Check-In Page – visit the student check-in page on your phone’s browser.
  3. Enter Your Details – type your student number and tap “Start Broadcasting”.
  4. Stay Connected – keep the page open so your attendance can be recorded.
